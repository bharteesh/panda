1Through the example of some French verbs called « to space potential »¹, built by derivational prefixation, I propose to show that the semantic and syntactic contribution of the operation of prefixation is constant whatever the time to which construction took place, whether units built into the system in Latin or French system.
2I will thus endeavor to present the case of the operation of prefixation in en-, in and their alternatives of form, starting from several verbs such as emmener, s'insérer, s'envoler, s'enfuir, s'engouffrer, etc.
3The analysis presented will thus testify, to a certain extent, of the stability of the system préfixai in French, at least with regard to the contribution of the prefix en, since the Latin time until today.
